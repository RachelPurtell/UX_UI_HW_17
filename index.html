Shel Skincare App

Shël is an app designed to bring clarity and ease to a person's skincare routines. Through Shël a user can build and organize routines with cosmetic products they are familiar with as well as discover new products rating high in compatibility to their skin type. Upon building their routines users can amplify consistency by using Shël’s daily progress tracker to mark their routines as complete.
